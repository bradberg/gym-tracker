Phase 1 Gym Tracker

More cardio, more core, more knee stability. Enough lifting to support it.

Lift A
M
Warmup Cardio
8 to 12 min
Easy to moderate.
Use elliptical, bike, treadmill walk, or rower. Warm up. Do not gas yourself before lifting.
M
Leg Press
3 x 10
Feet shoulder width. Lower until knees are bent comfortably. Press up smoothly. Keep low back against the pad.
M
Seated Row
3 x 10
Pull toward lower ribs. Chest up. Squeeze shoulder blades back. No body swing.
F
DB Romanian Deadlift
2 x 10
Soft knees. Push hips back. Lower dumbbells along thighs and shins with a flat back. Stand tall by driving hips forward.
H
Low Step-Ups
2 x 8 each leg
Use a low box. Step up. Stand tall. Step down under control. Hold support if needed.
H
Plank
3 rounds of 20 to 30 sec
Elbows under shoulders. Body straight. Squeeze abs and glutes. Stop before hips sag.

Lift B
M
Warmup Cardio
8 to 12 min
Easy to moderate.
Elliptical, bike, treadmill walk, or rower. Warm up first. It should not drain the workout.
F
Goblet Squat to Box / Bench
3 x 8 to 10
Hold one dumbbell at chest. Sit back to a box or bench. Lightly touch it and stand back up. Controlled quad work with some knee stability demand.
M
Lat Pulldown
3 x 10
Pull to upper chest. Chest up. Elbows down. No yanking.
M
Hamstring Curl
3 x 10 to 12
Curl with control. Slow on the way back. Hamstrings help support the knee.
H
Glute Bridge / Hip Thrust
2 x 12
Feet flat. Drive hips upward. Squeeze glutes. Avoid over-arching the low back. Strong glutes help with knee tracking.
H
Balance Board
2 to 3 rounds
Do this after the main work. Use support to get set, then let go and keep the board flat.
H
Dead Bug / Side Plank
2 to 3 rounds
Pick one. Dead bug for controlled core work. Side plank for side-core stability.

M
Warmup Cardio
8 to 12 min
Easy to moderate.
Steady elliptical or other low-impact cardio. Good place to add total conditioning without running.
H
Assisted Split Squat / Reverse Lunge
2 x 8 each leg
Hold support if needed. Stay controlled. Keep range of motion knee-friendly.
M

3 x 8 to 10
Incline press if open. Shoulder press if not. Enough upper work to stay balanced, but not the focus.

Pull to torso. Squeeze shoulder blades. Smooth reps only.

2 x 12
Use curl first if your knee likes it better. Extension is okay if it feels fine in the muscle and not the joint.

3 x 12 to 15
Up controlled, pause, down slow. Build tissue tolerance first. No explosive reps for now.
M
Pallof Press
2 to 3 sets
Stand sideways to the cable and press straight out. Resist rotation. Good core stability work.
